Tracking hardware, software assets, and license compliance accurately is challenging for any IT organization; people move and systems change. Virtualization only compounds the problem with the creation of on-demand resources which can quickly put you out of license compliance. Software vendors are aggressively imposing fines on firms who are found to be illegally using software (unintended or not).

As we move more to the cloud how are you controlling cost? How do you contain sprawl of your cloud instances? It is much easier to spin up a new VM that it is to purchase hardware.

If that critical overnight batch process goes down in the middle of the night who do you call? What is the business impact? Mapping the service relationship to each process becomes critical to define operational procedures, business owners and business impact.

SHAW experienced team have the hands-on experience to help automate the process to keep track of your assets, develop workflows to notify you of potential compliance violations and create dashboards to provide a picture of high-risk areas.
