Michael Moore,

Advisor

Mike has over 13 years of leadership experience at Cisco, most recently responsible for $700m Sales Operation in the Northeast, building strategic customer relationships with Enterprise clients. Mike’s passion for customer success and true business outcomes serve as the foundation for the positive and entrepreneurial culture his teams embrace.

Talent development is core to Mike’s approach to building world class teams. As companies evolve in the digital age, attracting and retaining top talent are essential to solving his client’s most important business challenges. A recognized organizational innovator, Mike’s teams have consistently led Cisco in growth, profitability, productivity and new business solutions (Cloud, IoT, Software and Services).

Mike graduated from Cornell University and is a former Naval Officer who served in the first Gulf War. He enjoys coaching and playing ice hockey.
